Quick Summary

Pixels Online is a play-to-earn farming and exploration game built on blockchain. This guide covers everything you need to start your journey: creating an account, understanding the basics, and taking your first steps toward earning $PIXEL tokens.

Introduction

Welcome to Pixels Online! Whether you're new to blockchain gaming or a seasoned player, this guide will help you understand the fundamentals and start earning from day one.

Pixels Online combines classic farming simulation with modern blockchain technology, allowing you to own your in-game assets and earn real cryptocurrency while playing.

Creating Your Account

Getting started with Pixels is simple:

  1. Visit the official website: Go to pixels.xyz
  2. Connect your wallet: You can use MetaMask, WalletConnect, or create a Pixels-managed wallet
  3. Complete the tutorial: Follow the in-game tutorial to learn basic controls
  4. Claim your starter items: You'll receive basic tools to begin your journey

Your First Steps

Once you're in the game, here's what to do first:

1. Complete the Tutorial Quests

The tutorial will teach you:

  • How to move around and interact with objects
  • Basic farming mechanics (planting and harvesting)
  • How to use your inventory
  • Where to find important locations

2. Start Farming

Farming is the foundation of earning in Pixels:

  • Plant your first crops (start with popberries - they're quick!)
  • Water and tend to your plants
  • Harvest when ready
  • Sell at the market or use for crafting

3. Explore the World

Visit different areas to unlock new opportunities:

  • Terravilla: The main hub with shops and services
  • Beach: Fishing and merchant ships
  • Forest: Woodcutting and foraging
  • Mines: Mining and stoneshaping

Understanding the Economy

Pixels has a dual-currency system:

Coins (In-Game Currency)

  • Earned through gameplay activities
  • Used to buy items, seeds, and tools
  • Can be converted to $PIXEL tokens

$PIXEL Tokens (Cryptocurrency)

  • The blockchain token of Pixels
  • Can be traded on exchanges
  • Used for NFT purchases and premium features
  • Earned through gameplay and staking
